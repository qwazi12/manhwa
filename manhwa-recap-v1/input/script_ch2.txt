Long ago, the fighters of Jianghu trained in martial arts for the sole purpose of defending themselves from their enemies. Over time, however, these protective practices began to change, slowly transforming into lethal tools used to kill their opponents. The discipline eventually became far more sophisticated and complex. Simple physical forms were woven into elaborate sequences, while breathing techniques came to represent the very foundation of internal energy.

Long ago, the ancient masters of combat struck the earth with devastating power, passing down their secret teachings and precious heirlooms to those who followed them. Because of this, their sacred martial arts never died out but kept evolving with each passing generation. 

Those who fully mastered these incredible techniques eventually became far more powerful than ordinary humans. Before long, society began to refer to these formidable individuals as the Moorim people.

Long ago, fighters who wished to become disciples of the Moorim people gathered together to form their own clans. They looked out over a vast and windy land, seeking their path. 

In time, these groups split into two opposing sides. The world became divided between the forces of justice and the forces of evil. 

One of the most formidable factions arose under a dark palace sky. People called this feared alliance the Demonic Cult. 

Within this powerful sect lived an illegitimate child born to the highest-ranking family. As he stood before his bowing subordinates, the boy knew that he held the rightful claim to the throne.

The boy stared straight ahead. Sweat poured down his face, but he refused to look away.

The boy bolted upright in bed with a sharp gasp. He was sweating heavily and noticed his clothes were covered in blood. The protagonist stared down at his hands in disbelief. He realized that he was still alive. This puzzled him, since he clearly remembered a sword piercing his stomach.

The protagonist wiped the sweat from his forehead as he looked down in shock. He wondered what had happened to him and why his entire body was sweating. Suddenly, the guy sat up in bed and clutched his mouth in pain. He coughed up blood onto his hands. The boy reached up and felt his hair. He pulled his hand away to find a sticky black substance clinging to his fingers. He stared at it and wondered what it was. The prince pulled open his robes and grimaced. He realized his entire chest was covered in the foul residue and noted that it smelled awful. It made him feel like throwing up, but then someone suddenly asked if he was awake.

A sudden, sharp crackle broke the silence. An unnatural sound echoed out into the emptiness as if a strange broadcast were about to begin.

The boy stared in complete shock. A voice had just asked if he was awake.

A sudden, sharp sound shattered the emptiness. It was a jarring, wordless crackle that seemed to split the very silence.

The blood-stained man recoiled in terror. He stared at a figure rising under a blanket and demanded to know who they were. The protagonist frantically searched his surroundings. He found no one there and desperately tried to calm himself. The guy froze. He realized that the mysterious voice was actually echoing directly inside his own head.

The protagonist recoiled in shock. He asked if the voice was coming from inside his own head. The entity confirmed his suspicion and addressed him as master. The boy gasped in disbelief. 

The guy clenched his fists in anger. He demanded to know who was speaking to him. The voice explained that it served as a core to support his brain. It identified itself as the seventh unit of Sky Corporation. 

The prince screamed in anguish. He asked what the voice was talking about. He demanded to know who they were and why they were doing this to him.

A sharp, sudden cry cut through the air. Someone let out a breathless gasp of shock.

The injured boy stood inside the wooden room and stared out the window. His robe was torn and covered in blood. He heard a voice mention a generation nano machine. The protagonist was confused and asked if they meant an evil god. The voice corrected the guy. It confirmed that it was indeed a nano machine.

The boy stared in sudden shock and began to sweat. He wondered if the text referred to the evil god whom the demonic cult worshipped.
