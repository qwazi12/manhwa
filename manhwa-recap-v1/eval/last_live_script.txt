The war with the labyrinth had raged for decades—a brutal struggle of humanity spilling blood to reclaim the surface from biomechanical horrors. While soldiers finally secured victory under the sun, the untamed depths remained sovereign. It was in those forbidden, pitch-black zones that the labyrinth claimed a strange prize of its own. Far beneath the ground, nursed by dark, gothic forces under a blood-red moon, a human infant was born and raised by the very monsters humanity feared.

Born deep within the untamed labyrinth during a brutal war, a child crackling with unstable blue energy was rescued and dubbed the Dungeon Baby. Years later, a greedy film crew and arrogant miners hired the now-grown guide to lead them through the dark caverns. Though he strictly warned them to avoid using firearms to prevent alerting predators, the guards mocked his caution. The moment they encountered a minor beast, they shot it dead, laughing off his fear and boasting about their easy payday, completely oblivious to the doom they had just summoned.

The Guide demanded they dump their excess gear and retreat immediately, but the arrogant miners only sneered, asking if the fearsome Dungeon Baby had wet himself. Their mocking laughter was instantly swallowed by a heavy vibration that rattled the cavern's purple stone walls. The miners froze as the deep rumbling grew louder, signaling that whatever lurked in the dark had finally answered their gunfire.

As a deep vibration rattled the cavern, the Guide warned that a massive predator had heard their gunshots. But the miners, blinded by the promise of a multi-million-won payday, refused to retreat and prepared to fight. Unwilling to die for their greed, the Guide abandoned them to their fate. He ordered the terrified film crew to follow him, ignoring the reporter’s protests about her ruined broadcast, and forced them to flee into the pitch-black tunnels. The hunt had begun, and those who stayed behind were already dead.

As distant gunshots and horrific screams echoed through the cavern, the reporter realized the terrible truth about their guide. When she frantically accused him of abandoning the guards to die, he coldly explained that the slain monster was mere bait. Trying to rescue the arrogant miners, who were already marked by the scent of blood, would have only doomed them all. He silenced her rising panic as the slaughter ground to its brutal end, knowing their own survival now depended on absolute silence.

The white-furred wolf monkeys descended on the stragglers with brutal speed, tearing through armor and flesh. The reporter screamed in terror, realizing these high-level beasts should never have been on the shallow first floor. Surrounded by the red-eyed pack, the remaining guards were instantly seized and crushed, leaving the survivors trapped. The guide realized the predators had tracked them from the very start. As a colossal ape loomed over him with bared fangs, crackling blue electrical energy ignited in the guide's eyes.

As the massive, white-furred wolf monkeys closed in, the Guide moved with blinding speed, flashing his blade to carve through the beasts before scooping up the terrified reporter and cameraman. He lunged across the collapsing cavern, escaping the roaring monsters just as the ground shattered behind them. Once safe, the hysterical reporter wept, demanding to know why he had let the miners die when he possessed such lethal strength. The Guide looked down at her with absolute indifference, reminding her that he was hired as a guide, not a guard, and that her arrogant crew had sealed their own fates by ignoring his every warning.

The Guide immediately demanded his payment, reminding the trembling cameraman that his contract was the only thing keeping them alive. But as drifting embers illuminated the cavern, their relief vanished. Carved into the stone before them was a Labyrinth Emblem. The panicked cameraman confessed he had fled blindly in any direction, only to realize the consequence of his terror too late. They had bypassed the safety boundaries entirely, leaving the upper floors behind to stand at the edge of the forbidden deep zones.

The stone floor shattered, plunging them into the dark abyss. The Guide awoke alone on the cavern floor, his left arm shattered. He barely had time to register the injury before a massive, coiled serpent constricted his body. Looking up into its glowing eyes, he realized too late how blindingly fast the predator truly was as its fanged jaws lunged to swallow him whole.
